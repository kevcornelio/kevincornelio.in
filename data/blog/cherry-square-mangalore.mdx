If you ever find yourself in Mangalore and need a place to sit down, slow down, and have a proper conversation — Cherry Square is that place.

I have been going there regularly for years, usually with Dr. Malcolm Pinto or Thimmaiah. It is the kind of spot where you can spend hours talking about life without feeling rushed.

**What to eat:** The sandwiches and snacks are good, but the star is "Some Like It Hot" — a sizzler ice cream that is exactly as interesting as it sounds. The burgers and sundaes are worth trying too, though I can never remember all the names on the menu.

**Ambiance:** Both open-air and air-conditioned seating are available. I always go for the open space — the aesthetics and the music make it worth it.

**Pricing:** Very reasonable. No surprises on the bill.

**Service:** Attentive without being intrusive — which is a harder balance to strike than most restaurants realise.

If your colleagues are planning a trip to Mangalore, put Cherry Square on the list. You will not regret it.

Not sure if it exists
Surely gives hope to people
Believers believe in it
Atheists laugh at it
Agnostics play around with it
An Excuse for stupidity
Pain reliever during tragedies
Reason for procrastination
Religions run on it
Poor people ponder over it
Optimists skip it
Pessimists fear it
Achievers are divided over it
Authors write on it
Astrologers read it
Not sure if it exists,
But surely keeps the world going!

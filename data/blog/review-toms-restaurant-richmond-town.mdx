I cannot believe it took me this long to visit — being a Mangalorean myself.

Tom's Restaurant is a must-visit for every Mangalorean living in Bangalore. I have only been once so far, but it was good enough to make me fall in love with the place immediately.

The Beef Vindaloo did it for me. Beyond that, the Sanna, Apa, Pork dishes, and Chicken Sukka are all highlights. I have promised myself to visit at least once a month and work my way through the menu.

The ambiance feels like an old-style classical Mangalorean home — pleasant, peaceful, and unpretentious. Prices are surprisingly reasonable. The location is convenient too.

Thumbs up from me. If you have not been, go.

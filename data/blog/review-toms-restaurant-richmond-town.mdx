Well I can't believe I hadn't been to this place earlier, being a Mangalorean. It's a must visit for every Mangalorean who's staying in Bangalore. Though I have been here only once but it was good enough for me to fall in love with this place. 'Beef Vindaloo' did it for me :) Sanna, Apa, Pork dishes, Chicken Sukka are some other highlights of this place. Well I have promised myself to visit this place at least once a month and explore more dishes. The ambiance is like an old style classical Mangalorean home, very pleasant and peaceful. Prices are very reasonable to my surprise. It's in a very good location as well. Thumbs up from me 😊

Amazing weekend spot with a great ambiance. Reasonable prices. Went with my wife for dinner and we both loved it. Food was really delicious and attention to detail was given. Hospitality was good considering this place is really crowded in the weekends. Opt for terrace for a romantic dinner.

Personal recommendation: Boiled Egg Croquette

They do it really well here 😊

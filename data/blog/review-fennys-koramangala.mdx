Amazing weekend spot with great ambiance and reasonable prices.

My wife and I went for dinner and thoroughly enjoyed it. The food was delicious and you could tell attention to detail had gone into the menu. The place gets crowded on weekends, but the hospitality was warm and attentive throughout.

**Must order:** The Boiled Egg Croquette. They do it really well here.

**Tip:** Ask for the terrace if you are going for a romantic dinner. It is worth it.

Would definitely go back.
